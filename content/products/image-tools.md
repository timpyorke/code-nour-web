  # Privacy Policy for Image Tools

  *Last Updated: February 14, 2026*

  ## 1. Introduction

  Welcome to Image Tools ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

  > **Key Point:** Image Tools is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your photos to our servers. All image processing happens locally on your device.

  ## 2. Information We Collect

  ### 2.1 Photos and Images

  - **What we access:** When you use Image Tools, you grant permission to access photos from your device's gallery.
  - **How we use it:** Images are processed entirely on your device for conversion, resizing, and cropping operations.
  - **What we DON'T do:** We do NOT upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers or third parties.
  - **Storage:** Processed images are saved only to your device's local storage at your direction.

  ### 2.2 Analytics Data

  We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve user experience:

  - App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
  - Device information (device model, operating system version, language)
  - General location (country/region level only, not precise location)
  - App crashes and performance data via Firebase Crashlytics

  **Note:** This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

  ### 2.3 Advertising Data

  Image Tools displays advertisements through Google AdMob:

  - AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  - Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  - This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance
  - You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

  ### 2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  - Your name, email address, or contact information
  - Login credentials (the app doesn't require accounts)
  - Precise location data
  - Your photos or any visual content from your images
  - Payment information (the app is free)

  ## 3. How We Use Information

  The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  - **Provide core functionality:** Process images locally on your device
  - **Improve the app:** Understand which features are most used
  - **Fix bugs:** Identify and resolve crashes and errors
  - **Display ads:** Show advertisements to support free app distribution
  - **Analyze trends:** Understand user behavior in aggregate (not individually)

  ## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  ### 4.1 Third-Party Services

  Image Tools integrates the following third-party services:

  - **Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)**
    - Purpose: App analytics and crash reporting
    - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

  - **Google AdMob**
    - Purpose: Display advertisements
    - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  ### 4.2 What We Do NOT Share

  - We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  - We do NOT share your photos with any third parties
  - We do NOT share personally identifiable information
  - We do NOT use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy

  ## 5. Data Storage and Security

  - **Local processing:** All image operations happen on your device
  - **No cloud storage:** We do not store your photos on our servers
  - **Analytics data:** Stored securely by Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption
  - **Security measures:** We follow best practices to protect data handled by third-party services

  ## 6. Your Rights and Choices

  ### 6.1 Photo Access

  - You can revoke photo library access at any time in your device settings
  - The app will request permission again when needed

  ### 6.2 Advertising

  - **iOS:** Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  - **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  - You will still see ads, but they won't be personalized

  ### 6.3 Analytics

  - You can disable analytics through Firebase by contacting us
  - Note: This may affect our ability to improve the app and fix bugs

  ### 6.4 Data Deletion

  - Delete processed images: Remove them from your device's gallery
  - Delete analytics data: Uninstall the app; anonymous data will age out over time
  - Since we don't collect personal information, there's no account or profile to delete

  ## 7. Children's Privacy

  Image Tools does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). The app is designed for general audiences. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

  ## 8. International Data Transfers

  Firebase and AdMob are operated by Google LLC, a U.S. company. Anonymous analytics and ad data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where Google operates. Google complies with applicable data protection frameworks.

  ## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  - Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  - Displaying a notification in the app (for material changes)
  - Posting the updated policy on this page

  Your continued use of Image Tools after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  ## 10. Permissions Explained

  ### Android Permissions

  - **READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:** To access photos for conversion and editing
  - **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:** To save processed images (Android 12 and below)
  - **INTERNET:** For displaying ads and sending analytics
  - **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE:** To check network connectivity for ads

  ### iOS Permissions

  - **Photo Library Access:** To select and save photos for conversion and editing
  - **Network Access:** For displaying ads and sending analytics (no prompt required)

  ## 11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data under the following legal bases:

  - **Consent:** Photo library access, advertising tracking
  - **Legitimate Interest:** Analytics for app improvement, crash reporting
  - **Contractual Necessity:** Providing the core image processing functionality

  ## 12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

  California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and request deletion of personal information. However, Image Tools does not collect personal information as defined by the CCPA. The anonymous analytics data we collect cannot be linked to you as an individual.

  ## 13. Contact Us

  If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  - **Email:** privacy@codenour.com
  - **Developer:** Codenour
  - **App Name:** Image Tools

  We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.

  **The Bottom Line:**

  - Your photos NEVER leave your device
  - We collect only anonymous usage statistics
  - We use Firebase Analytics and AdMob (Google services)
  - We do NOT collect personal information
  - You can opt out of personalized ads anytime
  - You control photo access permissions
